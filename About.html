About

---Mwangi Kirungo -the creative eye behind Mwakie Visuals- is a visual storyteller adept in natural light photography and videography. Primarily based in Ruiru, Kiambu County, After completeing my Bachelor's in Statisics at Kwavonza, i disovered an emptiness within and sought out to fill it. Immersing myself in photography, I discovered the power of freezing moments in time. Every click of the camera is a dance between the technical skill and artistic intuitio, capturing the essence of fleeting beauty.

My journey in photography began with a fascination for storytelling through images. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to capturing authentic emotions, I strive to create visual narratives that resonate with each individual client.
